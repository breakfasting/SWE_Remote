Ingredients

  • Trader Joe's Pizza Dough
  • Baby Arugula from Safeway
  • Small cubes of breakfast sausage or any sausage of your choice
  • Tomato Marinara
  • Yellow onion
  • Olive oil
  • Shredded Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni

Wayne's Pizza

Description

It's Wayne's Homemade Pizza with fresh ingredients

Directions

  1. Wipe a thin layer of olive oil on to the surface of the cast iron pan. Add in Pizza dough and use fingers to strech it out, let it rest til room temperature
  2. Add a even layer of tomato marinara and the shredded mozzarella cheese, put on toppings as you like!
  3. A slight drizzle of olive oil on top befor baking at 450F for 20 mins
  4. Cut and grab a bunch full of baby arugula on top, Enjoy!
