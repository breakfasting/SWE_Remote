Description

It's Wayne's Homemade Pizza with fresh ingredients

Ingredients

  • Trader Joe's Pizza Dough
  • Shredded Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni

Directions

  1. Add Pizza dough to cast iron pan
  2. Add toppings
  3. Bake at 450F for 20 mins
  4. Enjoy!
